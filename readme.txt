== Installation ==

To get started:

Option A: Watch our tutorial below:

<iframe width="854" height="480" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/oQ0AEALE9j8" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>


OR

Option B: Follow these steps below:

1. Upload the entire 'cpaleadcom-wordpress-plugin' folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory

2. Goto CPAlead.com and sign up as a publisher here: https://www.cpalead.com/get-started.php

3. Once logged into your CPAlead account, you will need to obtain your CPAlead Publisher ID and Wordpress Token to activate the plugin. This information can be found on the upper right corner of this page: https://www.cpalead.com/dashboard/banners/add.php under ‘Generate Wordpress Token’.

4. Within the ad plugin, select the type of ad you would like to create and insert your Wordpress Token and Publisher ID you obtained in step 3.

5. Setup a banner ad, interstitial ad, pop under ad, pushup ad or locker within the plugin with our easy to use ad creation wizard.

6. Place the ad on your Wordpress page from the Manage Ads menu. Don't worry, we have tutorial videos for each ad option!
